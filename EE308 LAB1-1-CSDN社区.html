👋Hi, I'm a embryonic code farmer who is still in college. I'm 20 years old this year. I'm a junior in automation at Fuzhou University. I still remember my first computer course when I was just in college. In that class, I was forced to change from high school students' thinking to college students' thinking. 

It was really cruel, because programming was too new and challenging for me, so my adaptation period was as long as half a semester. Now it's my junior year, as a novice who has never been exposed to code, I have learned four languages, written thousands of lines of code, completed two projects, participated in mathematical modeling competitions and image recognition courses, etc. 

👀As a junior student, it is also time for me to consider the future. On the question of which technology direction I want to develop, I found myself very interested in artificial intelligence and data analysis. First, let's talk about the direction of artificial intelligence. The project I am currently working on is face recognition system, and the automation major I learned is closely related to robots. This direction is very consistent with the development direction of science and technology in the world. In the future, I plan to be admitted as a graduate student of this major and continue to work in this field. The second is the direction of data analysis. I got acquainted with this direction in the second semester of my sophomore year in the mathematical modeling competition. Although the result of the competition was only the second in the school competition, I found myself very interested in data analysis in the process of preparing for the competition and the competition. How successful it is to integrate the disordered data into a concise and clear analysis report, I can experience the wisdom and progress of human beings, as well as the beauty of numbers.

In addition to the JAVA, C++and C languages I learned in school, I also learned Python language and MATLAB, SPSS, Excel, MPLAB and other software by myself during the project. In order to better develop in the direction I am interested in, I also need to master the following skills:

Ⅰ. artificial intelligence

· Basic requirements of mathematics: advanced mathematics, linear algebra, probability theory, mathematical statistics and stochastic processes, discrete mathematics, numerical analysis.

· Accumulation of algorithm requirements: artificial neural network, support vector machine, genetic algorithm, etc. Of course, algorithms are required in all fields, such as navigation and robot position environment mapping grand slam; Many general algorithms that require time to accumulate.

Ⅱ.  Data analysis

· Mathematical knowledge: statistical model, linear algebra

· Analysis tools: VBA

· Programming language: SQL query, Hadoop and Hive query

🌱This is an article on the software engineering course lab1. I hope that I can listen carefully to the course in the process of learning software engineering, experience the charm of software development, and hope that I can get 1000 lines of code in the visible results.
